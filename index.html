What is Invoicer X?

Invoicer X is your ultimate solution for creating fast, professional, and customizable invoices. Whether you're a freelancer, small business owner, or large enterprise, this app simplifies billing and financial management.

With intuitive design, multi-currency support, and features tailored for modern business needs — including QR codes and VAT compliance — Invoicer X helps you stay organized and get paid faster.

📲 How to Use Invoicer X

  1. Step 1: Download the app from Google Play.
  2. Step 2: Open the app and go to My Profile.
  3. Step 3: Select your preferred language before filling in your profile!
  4. Step 4: Fill in your company details and (optionally) add your logo.
  5. Step 5: Save your information by tapping the Save Profile button.
  6. Step 6: Go back to the Invoice section.
  7. Step 7: Enter client details: name, address, VAT number (if needed).
  8. Step 8: Add services, quantities, and prices for the invoice.
  9. Step 9: Tap the Generate Invoice button to create the invoice.
  10. Step 10: If needed, save the invoice as a PDF or print it.

⚠️ The invoice will not generate correctly if required fields are missing or incomplete!

📄 How to Create Your First Invoice – Quick Guide

  1. Install the App: Download Invoicer X from Google Play Store.
  2. Go to "My Profile": Change your language, add your business or personal info, and upload your logo.
  3. Save Your Information: Scroll to the bottom and tap "Save" to confirm your profile settings.
  4. Go to "Invoice": Add the client's name, address, OIB or B2B info, and the invoice date.
  5. Flat Rate Users: If you are using a flat rate, check the corresponding box.
  6. Non-Flat Rate Users: Add your service, quantity, currency, and price.
  7. Optional Note: You can add additional notes in the "Notes!" field if needed.
  8. Add More Items: To add more services or products, click "Add Item" and fill in the details.
  9. Generate Invoice: Tap "Generate Invoice" – the invoice will be dynamically created based on your input.
  10. Save and Print: Save the generated invoice as a PDF and print it if needed.
  11. Find Saved Invoices: All saved invoices are located in your device's Downloads folder.

✅ Tip: Make sure to complete your profile first so the invoice includes your correct business info and logo.

🧾 Reminder: You can reuse client info for future invoices to save time.
